Hello, I'm Sarah

Welcome to NomNomList! I'm a passionate food enthusiast, home cook, and recipe developer based in Portland, Oregon. My culinary journey began in my grandmother's kitchen when I was just tall enough to reach the counter.

After spending 5 years working in various restaurants and completing culinary school, I decided to share my love for food through this blog. NomNomList is where I document my culinary adventures, share my favorite recipes, and connect with fellow food lovers around the world.

My cooking philosophy is simple: use fresh, seasonal ingredients and let them shine. I believe that cooking should be fun, approachable, and result in delicious meals that bring people together.
